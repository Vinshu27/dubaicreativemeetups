🌿 The circle

A space to relax, express yourself & meet lovely humans

Let's turn ordinary evenings into something a little more meaningful.

Dubai Creative Meetups is a community for anyone in Dubai looking to slow down, get creative, and connect. Whether you're into mandala painting, book-and-coffee discussions, art jams, or just chill social events — this is your space.

New to the city? Want to try something different? Come join the circle. Every session is small, warm and welcoming — you'll leave with something you made and people you actually talked to.

No experience needed Solo-friendly Open to all Good vibes only
🎨 What we do

Pick your kind of evening

From brushes to books to board-game banter — there's more than one way to get creative with us.

Fan favourite
🍷

Paint & Sip

Canvas, coffee (or something bubblier), and a relaxed guided session in the cutest settings. Walk in empty-handed, walk out with a masterpiece.

🌸

Mandala Art

Slow, meditative dot-and-pattern painting. Deeply calming, endlessly satisfying — and gorgeous on your wall when you're done.

📚

Book & Coffee Circles

Cozy conversations over good coffee — books, ideas and stories with people who love to talk about more than the weather.

🖌️

Art Jams

Freestyle creative sessions — no theme, no rules. Bring your ideas, borrow our supplies, and see where the evening takes you.

👜

Craft & DIY

Tote-bag painting, wall art, seasonal makes — hands-on sessions where you create something you'll actually use.

🎉

Socials & Private Events

Themed get-togethers, Galentine's specials, and private gatherings for birthdays and teams. Your people + our setup = a lovely time.
